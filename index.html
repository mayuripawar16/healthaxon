Who we are

HealthAxon is a UK based organisation which make investments in happiness. We quantify both
social and financial returns generated by our holdings, with a portfolio made up of sustainable Health
and Social Care services. Our clients include Local Authorities, the NHS and private individuals.

We think holistically about returns and believe in creating durable, scalable impact based on our
values whilst aligning stakeholders.
